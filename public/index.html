About the YaadVibe Ecosystem

The 1Drop YaadVibe Ecosystem is Jamaica’s first tourism-powered, blockchain-driven curator engine. Visitors and locals connect to real businesses, events, services, and attractions through blockchain-secured bookings, $JamDex payments, Tezos SBT identity, and an AI metadata engine that learns user preferences across all four DApp/App experiences.

YaadVibe brings tourism, culture, and commerce together in one frictionless interface — real-time updates, instant payments, and curated recommendations powered by your SBT identity.
