About the YaadVibe Ecosystems

YaadVibe

YaadVibe is the premiere flagship DApp — the core engine powering the entire ecosystem. Featuring a full interactive UI, real-time accommodations, transportation, on-chain automation, identity, and proof of payments the first of its kind of all other web3 products available.

YaadLife Lite

A fast, secure TON-powered version of YaadLife featuring SBT identity, credit/debit, and crypto payments. All core utilities — lighter, faster, and optimized for the TON ecosystem.

PurVibe

PurVibe transforms playlists and subscriptions into fully-scheduled AI-curated entertainment channels. AI programs content into time-based streams, recreating the feel of visual or audio channels — driven by automation and personalization.

IrieVibe

A real-time booking assistant for Jamaicans — available through Telegram or as a standalone app. From food orders and reservations to transportation and accommodations, using platforms people already use every day. Full on-chain payment system that allows credit debit and JamDex.
