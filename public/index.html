About YaadVibe

YaadVibe is a decentralized travel ecosystem (DApp) built to highlight the beauty, culture, and experiences across Jamaica’s 14 parishes. Visitors can explore accommodations, tours, food, attractions, transportation, and community experiences — all powered by blockchain payments including WalletConnect, TONConnect, JamDex, and more.
