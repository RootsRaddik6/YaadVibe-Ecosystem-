About the YaadVibe Ecosystem

YaadVibe

YaadVibe is the premiere flagship DApp — the core engine powering the entire ecosystem. Featuring a full 3D interactive map UI, real-time APIs, on-chain automation, identity, and the foundation from which all other YaadVibe applications evolve.

YaadLife Lite

A fast, secure TON-powered version of YaadLife featuring SBT identity, credit/debit, and crypto payments. All core utilities — lighter, faster, and optimized for the TON ecosystem.

PurVibe

PurVibe transforms playlists and subscriptions into fully-scheduled AI-curated television channels. It programs your content into time-based streams, recreating the feel of classic TV — driven by automation and personalization.

IrieVibe

A real-time local discovery and booking assistant for Jamaicans — available through Telegram or as a standalone app. From food orders and reservations to transport and accommodations, using platforms people already use every day.
